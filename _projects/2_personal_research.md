## Overview

This project represents the kind of self-directed research page that adds technical depth beyond the main industry project.

## Research Question

Describe one clear technical question.
Examples include a modeling choice, a validation procedure, a controller comparison, or a data-driven method.

## Method

Document the workflow in a way that is easy to review later:

- problem setup
- method or algorithm
- datasets or simulation conditions
- evaluation criteria

## What I Contributed

Be explicit about what was built or tested directly in this study.
The goal is to make your contribution legible even to someone who has never seen the underlying thesis or lab notes.

## Results

Summarize what changed after running the experiments, comparisons, or simulations.
If the result produced a useful figure, poster section, or technical note, link it here.

## Notes for the final version

Keep this page tighter than a full thesis chapter.
It should be detailed, but still scannable in a few minutes.
